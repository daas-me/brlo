About the Office

The Business Registration & Licensing Office (BRLO) is a simulated government office created as a supplementary component of the Entrepreneurship 1 subject of Grade 12 at Central Mindanao University Laboratory High School.

The BRLO manages the simulated business registration and permitting process for all student business groups across the four Grade 12 sections — Archimedes, Aristotle, Galileo, and Kepler. Each group must secure a Business Permit before they are considered officially registered and may begin operations.

This online registry serves as the public record of all approved businesses for S.Y. 2025–2026. All permits are issued under the supervision of the subject instructor and remain valid until the end of May 2026.
